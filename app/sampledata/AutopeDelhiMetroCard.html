Autope Delhi Metro Card

No more waiting in long queues and tapping your cards at AVM

Link your bank account online and avail auto top-up facility at entry gates itself. As and when the value in the card falls below Rs.100, it will automatically be recharged with Rs. 200 at the Automatic Fare Collection (AFC) entry gates itself. Autope will auto-debit your linked card/bank account with the topped up value.


Benefits:

  • No need to track usage of the card
  • No need to recharge it every time
  • 15% discount on Delhi metro fare
  • Home delivery of cards
  • Customisation of cards

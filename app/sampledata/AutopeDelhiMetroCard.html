Autope Delhi Metro Card

To offer a hassle-free and seamless recharge experience “Autope” in collaboration with DMRC brings you the ‘Autope Delhi Metro Card’ to provide a revolutionary facility to auto top-up your metro card on the go. Whenever your card is scanned at the Automatic Fare Collection (AFC) entry gates and found with insufficient balance to travel, it will be auto-recharged with the pre-defined value at the AFC gate itself.

“Autope” will fund these transactions and ‘Auto Debit’ your bank/card account after a certain period of time.


Features of Autope Delhi Metro Card:

  • No need to wait in long queues for recharges at metro stations.
  • Home delivery of cards.
  • 10% discount on delhi metro fare.
  • Single transaction limit for recharge is Rs. 200
  • Multiple top ups can be done to exhaust the mandate limit over a specific period of time.
  • One time bill payment for all the recharges consumed during the credit period.

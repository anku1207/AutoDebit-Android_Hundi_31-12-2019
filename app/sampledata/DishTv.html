Autope Dish TV Recharge

‘Now Dish TV subscribers can enjoy uninterrupted viewing of their favourite programs without worrying about manually recharging their DTH every month’


Dear user,“Dish TV” has partnered with us to offer the “Autope” feature to you.

This feature allows easy DTH recharges of “Dish TV” for “Autope” consumers by facilitating automatic payments through a one-time mandate.

Once the “Autope” feature is enabled, it ensures that a user’s “Dish TV” account is automatically recharged three days before the due date every month.

“Autope” will fund these transactions and ‘Auto Debit’ your bank/card account after a certain period of time.

Features of Autope Dish TV Recharge:

  • Autope enables greater convenience for you.
  • Autope offers hassle-free and seamless recharge experience.
  • No need to worry about monthly manual recharges.
